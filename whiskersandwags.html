Wayside Waifs Retail Store

So you’re getting ready to adopt! Congratulations! You’re going to need a few things – don’t worry you can get everything you need at Wayside in our Whiskers & Wags Retail Store. You could save 10-20% off items like collars, toys, litterboxes, litter, treats, beds, training crates and so much more!

WHY shop at Whiskers & Wags? Every purchase goes right back into our shelter to rescue more homeless pets like the one you are adopting! Your purchase provides life-saving medical care, food and safe shelter to 6,000 homeless pets.

Whiskers provides stress free shopping from the big box stores! No long lines, large selection and a highly trained staff who will help you fit the collar or harness to your new furry family member! It's one stop shopping that saves lives.

We understand this is an exciting day and can help you through the process of selecting the things you need before heading home. No extra stops needed so you can get right home and start playing with your new furry family member!
