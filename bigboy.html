About Big Boy!

Hi there! I'm Big Boy!

Even though my name is BIG boy, I am a medium size, around 45 pounds right now. I recently came to Wayside Waifs on a transfer and I am now looking for my happily ever after!

I am a sweet, friendly fella! I am smart too! I already know the commands SIT and LAY DOWN and am ready and willing to learn more! I am praise and treat motivated, too. Maybe my name should be GOOD boy?

I walk nicely on leash and don't pull much at all. I would love to go on hikes with you or even just play around in the backyard.
It's just that I believe we both deserve the best and purrhaps that means we deserve each other!

I sure hope I can be your best fur friend for life!
